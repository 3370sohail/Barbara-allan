ARTIST STATEMENT

"I use photography as a means of telling a story. I am drawn to detail, places and moments in time. I appreciate simplicity, things historical, the joy and beauty of nature and the majesty and inspiration of fine architecture. I am captivated by the experiences of those who have gone before and left us with things to ponder on, to wonder about - pieces of themselves that continue to enrich our present day experience. Light, darkness and silence, in all their myriad forms, guide me, encourage me and compel me to see with my camera and hold moments in time."

VIEW ARTIST BIO

Barbara Allan was born, raised and still resides in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. She has a Bachelor of Fine Arts from Western University in London, Ontario and a Bachelor of Education from the University of Toronto with a specialization in fine arts and history. Barbara has been a visual arts teacher and guidance counsellor with the Peel Board of Education for many years and currently is an Engagement Teacher with the Peel Board specializing in at-risk youth. Barbara has been taking photographs throughout Europe and North America for over 35 years. Photography has been a great passion in her life and is her second career. Barbara started using a camera as a teenager to capture images she wanted to paint but soon became enamored with the medium, realizing that the camera lens could be her canvas. Her fine art photographs have been exhibited at a number of venues throughout Toronto and in Northern Ontario.

    FINE ART PORTRAITS

    A session fee covers the shoot itself, with no obligation to purchase images. Session fees start at $100.00.

    Where - Your session can take place wherever you like: in your home or cottage, a park, anywhere familiar and comfortable for you and your family

    Time-of-Day - I perfer to use as much natural light as possible to maintain the integrity of the moment we are trying to capture; therefore, morning and afternoon sessions are best for outdoor shooting, morning and early afternoon for indoor shooting.
